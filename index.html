Mevo

September 2014 - Present

Founding Director // Head of Product

Wellington, NZ

Mevo is Australasia's first free-floating car share, launched in Wellington in late 2016. Mevo provides on-demand access to shared fleets of vehicles to registered Members via proprietary mobile apps. Mevo Members can find, reserve, lock and unlock cars using the apps, and only pay for the time they use.

Along with my excellent team, we're scaling a disruptive, high-growth business that is leveraging trends in emerging and exponential technology while delivering a desirable, world-class user experience.
