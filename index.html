My strengths lie in my logical thought process and attention to detail, which give me great capacity to solve complicated problems. I learn quickly and always work towards making validated progress that can be well communicated. When I'm not at the office or working on my next idea for a startup, I'm reading up on the latest tech or learning something new. I travel whenever I can, with as little luggage as possible, and like to avoid the typical tourist grind. In the rest of my spare time I write code, take photos, and drink good beer.
